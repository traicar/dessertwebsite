Welcome Sweet Lovers!

Explore a world of cakes, cupcakes, cookies, treats and more at our online store. It is a one stop shop to meet your dessert needs.

Services We Offer

We offer online ordering for in store pick up for your immediate dessert needs. We also cater for birthdays, weddings, office parties or any other reason to celebrate that you can think of. You can choose from our delicious menu or get creative! We aim to satisfy all tastebuds! You know what they say, all's well that ends sweet!

COVID-19 protocol

For the protection of our staff and customers, we have moved to online ordering only.If you miss our smiling faces, you can still pick up your order at the store once you order online. Well, our smiles might be hidden behind the masks though. Or else, just sit back, relax and wait for your desserts to be delivered-no-contact at your doorstep by our friendly staff.Saves you time and from that nasty virus! We are accepting phone consultations for customized orders. We will deliver tasting boxes so you can finalize on the flavors.
