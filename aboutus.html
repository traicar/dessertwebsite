We are happy you found us!

Hi there!

Thank you for visiting our page and wanting to know more about us. We are a small family owned business, operated by two sisters, Ana and Elsa. The joy for backing started early when we baked with our parents for family gathering and on weekends. As we grew up this hobby of baking with family grew into a passion. With family and friends appreciating our baked goods, we decided to start a small from home business for additional income. We baked from home for around 2 years but as the word spread, we started getting more orders than we could handle. We always knew at the back of our minds that we wanted a bakery or a dessert shop of our own but had never discussed it.

On Christmas of 2018, our dad casually said, as he enjoyed the Christmas cake, you gals should think about going big! We looked at each other and knew that we both had the same dream.We started discussing this seriously and 5 months later, here we are, two proud owners of sweeTreats.Our passion is to share with our community our joy of baking. We strive to make your joyous occasion more fun and memorable with our delicious treats. Besides our crowed favorite and regular menu items, we meet with our clients personally and try to customize our baked goods for their palate. We love a delicious challenge!So come to our store and try what we have to offer for your sweet tooth. We can bet you will leave with a happy tummy!

Elsa and Ana
